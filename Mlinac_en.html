Ivan - My Life in Music and Society

About Me

  My name is Ivan, born in 2006 and living in Karlovac, a beautiful city in Croatia.

Passion for Music

  From a young age, music has been my greatest interest and passion. Currently, I attend the Karlovac Music School, where I refine my skills daily and broaden my musical horizons.

Multiple Musical Talents

  I play several instruments, including double bass, saxophone, and piano, and I also have a background in various other instruments. My love for music is not limited to classical instruments; for the past two years, I have been dedicated to DJing and music production, exploring modern music styles and creating my own melodies.

Education and Creative Path

  After completing music high school in Karlovac, many doors have opened for my creative path. I am ready for any project that will allow further development and expression of artistic passion.

Cultural Heritage and Music

  I am a proud member of the Matija Gubec folklore ensemble, which is part of CIOFF, an international organization that promotes folk art and tradition. In this ensemble, I have the opportunity to combine my love for music with cultural heritage, representing our rich Croatian tradition on domestic and international stages.

Interests Beyond Music

  Besides music, I am interested in politics and law. I actively participate in local initiatives and projects aimed at improving the community and promoting justice. I believe it is important to be an informed and engaged citizen, as this is how we can build a better future for all.

Purpose of the Page

  On this page, I want to share my experiences, projects, and passions with you. I hope you will enjoy my musical journey and perhaps find inspiration for your own interests and goals.
