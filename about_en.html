Who Am I?

About Loris(LowKey)

  Welcome to my corner of the musical world! I'm Loris, a producer and violinist from the city of Karlovac.

Melodic journey

  My musical journey began when I was 11 years old. Since then, I have been constantly exploring different genres and pushing the boundaries of sound. I have ten years of experience playing the violin and engaging in production behind me.

The producer with a violin

  After delving deeper into classical music, I decided to connect it with contemporary styles. One of the main aspects of my musical exploration is simply experimenting with different genres such as trap, hip-hop, and rap. That's how I started creating my own pieces, of course with the help of experts.

From the stage to the studio

  Although the stage has been my playground during numerous concerts, not only at Music School Karlovac but also at the Folk ensemble Matija Gubec, the place where true magic happens is actually my studio. Here, I pour my heart and soul into creating music that not only moves the feet but also stirs emotions. Now, I'm venturing into the digital world to share my music with a wider audience. Through this website and social media, I hope to connect with other musicians, share my creations, and collaborate on exciting new projects.

Outside of music

  You'll often find me indulging in my other passions. Whether I'm casting a line while fishing, exploring new trails on my bike, or simply spending time with friends, I believe in fully embracing life and finding inspiration in every moment. I'm someone who looks at every aspect of life with a positive outlook. There are many things I love to do, see, and experience. I love to read, I love to play music; I love to think, I love to sing; I love to talk, but even more, I love to listen.

Let's make music together

  Join me in exploring creative possibilities together. Whether you're a fellow musician, a music enthusiast, or simply someone who appreciates a good rhythm, there's always room for you in my life. Thank you for visiting. Let's create something amazing together!
