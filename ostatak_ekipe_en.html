Bruno

Hello everyone! My name is Bruno, and my life's passion is art in all its forms. Born and raised in the beautiful city of Karlovac, Croatia, I have dedicated most of my life to music and photography. My musical adventure began ten years ago when I first picked up a clarinet. Its melody became an inseparable part of my life. Through years of practice, the clarinet became my best friend. Four years ago, I discovered another passion - the guitar. Its chords added a new dimension to my musical story. With the clarinet and guitar, the doors to the musical world opened, allowing me to freely explore different genres and create music. Photography is another love that found its place in my life three years ago. Through the camera lens, I find beauty in everyday scenes and turn them into lasting memories. Each photograph tells its own story. After graduating from the music high school in Karlovac, many doors opened on my creative journey. I am ready for any project that will allow me to further develop and express my artistic passion. In my free time, I love to travel and explore new destinations that inspire my artistic creation. I enjoy listening to and playing older rock hits, but I am always open to new musical genres. I carry a love for art in my heart and the readiness to share my talents with the world. Let music and images be the bridges that connect people and create magical moments that will last forever.
